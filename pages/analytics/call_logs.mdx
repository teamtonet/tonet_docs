# Call Logs

The call logs section displays a record of all the calls made, received, or missed on your Tonet phone system. You can also listen to each call's recording.

## Accessing Call Logs

To go to call logs, click on Manage from your Tonet dashboard.

![manage](https://i.imgur.com/8fmfOmS.jpg)

Click on call logs.

![call_logs](https://i.imgur.com/g4k1Xuk.jpg)



You will be prompted with this screen.

![screen](https://i.imgur.com/09VBfJ0.jpg)



You will find the size of your call recordings so far.

![volume](https://i.imgur.com/1UOaoAH.jpg)



You will find all the information pertaining to a call listed in the call logs, such as:

![call](https://i.imgur.com/XHfieUe.jpg)

* Direction: Whether it was a dialed call (upwards facing arrow) or a received call (downward facing arrow).

​	Example of a dialed call.

![dialed](https://i.imgur.com/Qz5b8iu.jpg)

​	Example of a received call.

​	![received](https://i.imgur.com/8rS2Txb.jpg)

* Source: The number that you made the call from.
* Destination: The number that you dialed.
* Date: The date of the call.
* Answered: If the call was answered, a red phone will be displayed.

![redphone](https://i.imgur.com/JIeWuXR.jpg)

​	If it wasn't answered, the cell will be empty.

![empty](https://i.imgur.com/HNSQseJ.jpg)

* Billing Duration: This is the duration of the call that you will be billed.
* Audio: This is the recording of the call.

You also have the option to delete any of the calls, just select the call, and click Delete Audio.

![delete](https://i.imgur.com/bWVpVkF.jpg)

You can also sort any of the cells by clicking on them.

![sort](https://i.imgur.com/2ez48r7.jpg)

You can also use filters by month.

![filters](https://i.imgur.com/8zVQeOX.jpg)

Checking the duration will display the full duration of the call.

![duration](https://i.imgur.com/S6yppT4.jpg)



Checking the source extension will display the source extension of the source (if found).

![source](https://i.imgur.com/i3U89h3.jpg)



Checking the destination extension will display the destination extension of the destination (if found).

![destination](https://i.imgur.com/1WLfV9l.jpg)



## Listening to Call Recordings

To listen to a call recording, click on the blue audio button.

![bluebutton](https://i.imgur.com/9M2A9wx.jpg)



The audio player will automatically start playing the record.

![audioplayer](https://i.imgur.com/W56fM1r.jpg)



You can change the audio volume from here.

![volume2](https://i.imgur.com/hKq83gC.jpg)

If you click on the three buttons, you will be able to download the record or change the playback speed.

![download](https://i.imgur.com/QZo316W.jpg)
