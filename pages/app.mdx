# Tonet.app

This is the main application where you can:

1. Make and receive calls from a web browser
2. Send SMS and receive SMS
3. Listen to voicemails
4. Edit your user profile
5. Choose from outbound text or call numbers permitted by system manager (from .io)
6. Send messages to other users in same company

## Logging In

From your browser, go to [tonet.app](https://tonet.app/)

You will be prompted with the log in screen.

![login](https://i.imgur.com/poqKUMD.jpg)

Enter your credentials and click Login.

### Forgot Password

In case you forgot your password, click Forgot Password?

![forgotpassword2](https://i.imgur.com/SSElpsy.jpg)

Enter your email address, and click send email.

![forgotpassword](https://i.imgur.com/YBXz8RG.jpg)

You will get a confirmation asking you to check your email for resetting your password.

![resetpassword](https://i.imgur.com/iHCfboj.jpg)

You will get an email (make sure that you check your spam messages as well) providing you with link to reset your password, click on the link to set up your new password.

![linktoreset](https://i.imgur.com/ZDmTrBw.jpg)

Enter your new password, and then repeat the password once more, and click set password.

![setpassword](https://i.imgur.com/D1EczeU.jpg)

Your will be prompted with the login screen, with your new password.

![login](https://i.imgur.com/poqKUMD.jpg)

Click login.

## Messages/SMS

Click on the messages button.

![messages](https://i.imgur.com/DA2rLxG.jpg)

![messages_screen](https://i.imgur.com/82Q8TMn.jpg)

### Sending an SMS to a Team Member

To send an SMS to a team member, click Add.

![addteam](https://i.imgur.com/idwPAO0.jpg)


Click the drop down menu.

![dropdown](https://i.imgur.com/sevYEU2.jpg)

Select the team member that you wish to text.

![addteammember](https://i.imgur.com/epTmStD.jpg)

A new window will open where you can text the team member that you selected.

![textteammember](https://i.imgur.com/DQ1ACTZ.jpg)


You can check the history of messages of a team member from here.

![history](https://i.imgur.com/w07GKOq.jpg)

To create a group SMS, select the first member from the add button.

![addteam](https://i.imgur.com/idwPAO0.jpg)


Select the first recipient.

![addteammember](https://i.imgur.com/epTmStD.jpg)

And then when the window of the recipient opens, click add again.

![addanother](https://i.imgur.com/e5eHLPK.jpg)


Select the second recipient.

![second](https://i.imgur.com/xNQFbhy.jpg)

Now you've created a group of two participants.

![two](https://i.imgur.com/p5BDrHq.jpg)

To send a message, click the send button.

![send](https://i.imgur.com/Xkq0v44.jpg)


You can also call the team member from the call button.

![call](https://i.imgur.com/Om6rUKF.jpg)


### Sending an SMS to Any Number

To send an SMS to any number, click Contacts.

![contacts](https://i.imgur.com/s9qhmaQ.jpg)

Click the add button to add a new number.

![addnewnumber](https://i.imgur.com/Rq7uMx1.jpg)

Enter the number that you wish to send an SMS to.

![enternumber](https://i.imgur.com/WWWNRIB.jpg)

Once you entered the number, a new window will open, where you can text the number.

![numberadded](https://i.imgur.com/jEbKlGt.jpg)


Next, choose the number that you're going to send the SMS from.

![sender](https://i.imgur.com/0CXAkAx.jpg)


Select the number, you can buy phone numbers from your dashboard on tonet.io

![selectnumber](https://i.imgur.com/ExBUq8s.jpg)

Type your message, and click send.

![send2](https://i.imgur.com/ptE6vC0.jpg)


On the left hand side, you will find all the history of all the SMS that you sent.

![records](https://i.imgur.com/sRmk9YK.jpg)

To send a new SMS, click clear.

![clear](https://i.imgur.com/vnBPCMF.jpg)


To call the number instead of sending an SMS, click the call button.

![call2](https://i.imgur.com/lYyV0Vw.jpg)
