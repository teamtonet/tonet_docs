# Calendar

The calendar feature allows you to mark certain dates and divert calls to a certain IVR, group, user, or a busy tone, this feautre is useful during holidays like Christmas where you can set an IVR to inform your clients you're going to take a number of days off and you will be returning to the office at a certain day.

## Create a Calendar  

To create a calendar, click Manage from the side menu.

![manage](https://i.imgur.com/Ol6WdsZ.jpg)

Click on Calendar.

![calendar](https://i.imgur.com/Zn6irgu.jpg)

You will be prompted with this screen.

![screen](https://i.imgur.com/niqwapt.jpg)

You can name your new calendar here.

![christmas](https://i.imgur.com/6B6Iudm.jpg)

Next, select the date, click from to set the beginning of the calendar.

![from](https://i.imgur.com/Nmwmn6x.jpg)

Select the date.

![date](https://i.imgur.com/an9vWWt.jpg)

Select the time.

![time](https://i.imgur.com/GdiJaOs.jpg)

And click ok.

![ok](https://i.imgur.com/7ZTvuBy.jpg)

Now set the end of the calendar, click on To.

![to](https://i.imgur.com/ZRNUpl4.jpg)

Select the date and time and click ok.

![todate](https://i.imgur.com/hQ4QW2F.jpg)

Next, click the destination.

![destination](https://i.imgur.com/u7vLlVx.jpg)

Select one of the destinations.

![destinationwhere](https://i.imgur.com/IbpQxTT.jpg)

You can choose one of the following destinations:

- Busy tone
- Divert to a certain group
- Divert to a certain IVR
- Diver to a certain user

In this case, you can [create a Christmas IVR](https://docs.tonet.ai/auto_attendant#set-up-auto-attendant) and set it as the destination.

Once you are done, click create.

![create](https://i.imgur.com/boHtGsn.jpg)

You will find your created calendar on the right hand side.

![done](https://i.imgur.com/TiwfXSF.jpg)
