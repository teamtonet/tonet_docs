# Billing

The billing section displays your billing details such as payment history, your usage, and a section to top up your account.

## Recharge Your Account

To recharge your account, go to Account.

![account](https://i.imgur.com/qErm6gU.jpg)


Click on Billing.

![billing](https://i.imgur.com/I88q5Ou.jpg)

You will be prompted with this screen.

![billingdetails](https://i.imgur.com/5MpWJQm.jpg)



This section allows you to manage your cards and top-up.

![cards](https://i.imgur.com/CT6z9vb.jpg)

To view your cards, click View Cards.

![viewcards](https://i.imgur.com/3b6ObXN.jpg)

To add a new card, click Add Card.

![addcard](https://i.imgur.com/7z5kyV5.jpg)

Enter the amount that you want to top up with, note that the minimum amount is $10 USD.

![enteramount](https://i.imgur.com/jVmo0N2.jpg)

If you wish to be automatically recharged, toggle on the button, otherwise, leave it off.

![autorecharge](https://i.imgur.com/onLXJPK.jpg)

When you switch it on, two fields will be displayed, the first one is the balance to trigger automatic recharge, for example, if you set it to $1, when your balance falls to $1, your account will be automatically recharged.

![fallsto](https://i.imgur.com/ajT8EMS.jpg)

The second field is the amount that you want to recharge to.

![rechargeto](https://i.imgur.com/sOd1BqN.jpg)

When you are ready, click Top Up.

![topup](https://i.imgur.com/BBld3x2.jpg)

## Usage

You can check your usage from the usage section.

![usage](https://i.imgur.com/FtshOnv.jpg)

This will display the date of the usage, the type, and the amount deducted.

You can also save the records in a spreadsheet, click Save to do that.

![save](https://i.imgur.com/SiV9Ry5.jpg)

A spreadsheet will automatically be downloaded.

You will find all the records organized in the spreadsheet.

![excel](https://i.imgur.com/iuyoNLe.jpg)

## Usage Chart

You can also check the usage in a chart style.

![chart](https://i.imgur.com/eBE6lUh.jpg)

Each color represents an element.

![elements](https://i.imgur.com/uzmuWOg.jpg)

You can click on an element to exclude it from the costing chart.

![exclude](https://i.imgur.com/jb7EDGb.jpg)

You can also check the last months' costs from here.

![lastmonths](https://i.imgur.com/PK5Ec2D.jpg)



## Payment History

Here you can check your payment history.

![paymenthistory](https://i.imgur.com/G0xz9ns.jpg)

You will find the date of the payment and the amount charged.

You can also save these records in a spreadsheet, click Save to do that.

![save2](https://i.imgur.com/yCDPFFX.jpg)
