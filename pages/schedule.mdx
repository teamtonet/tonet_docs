# Schedule

The schedule feature allows you to set up a weekly schedule for your phone system, you will be able to direct the calls during certain days (for example weekdays) to a certain feature, group, user, or IVR during certain hours (for example, like during business hours), and set another schedule during certain days (for example weekends) to direct to a certain feature, group, user, or IVR.

## Create a Weekday Schedule

To create a weekday schedule, click on manage.

![manage](https://i.imgur.com/DKfagij.png)

Click on Schedule.

![schedule](https://i.imgur.com/PjO9G4T.png)

Click on New Schedule.

![newschedule](https://i.imgur.com/R31IZu9.png)

Rename the schedule and click Add.

![name](https://i.imgur.com/ITS8oWg.png)

You will be prompted with this screen.

![screen](https://i.imgur.com/fk2kFlx.png)

Enter the time that this schedule will begin, click From.

![from](https://i.imgur.com/BreTXHm.png)

And set the ending time of this schedule, click To.

![to](https://i.imgur.com/sziJhxR.png)

Select the destination of the schedule, and click Go To.

![destination](https://i.imgur.com/eW4NIgc.png)

Select the days of this schedule.

![days](https://i.imgur.com/jUbTVQI.png)

And when you're done, click Save.

![save](https://i.imgur.com/4Xsl0ZW.png)

Your schedule has been created successfully.

![done](https://i.imgur.com/YTee0Wc.png)
