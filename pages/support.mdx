# Support

The support tab allows you to create a new ticket and have customer service reach out to you to solve the issue.

## Create a New Ticket	

From your Tonet dashboard, click on the support icon.

![support](https://i.imgur.com/fwEVz7U.jpg)

Click on New Ticket.

![newticket](https://i.imgur.com/P0Zxq9Z.jpg)

You will be prompted with this screen.

![screen](https://i.imgur.com/r4Er5wG.jpg)

Select the ticket type.

![ticket type](https://i.imgur.com/CF0IkwB.jpg)

Check out this [guide](https://docs.tonet.io/phones/port_number) if you want to port your number(s).

Enter the ticket subject.

![ticket subject](https://i.imgur.com/Kf9Q0zm.jpg)

Enter a description of the issue you are facing.

![ticket message](https://i.imgur.com/MZloMe2.jpg)

When you are ready to send the ticket, click submit.

![submit](https://i.imgur.com/gZ6061m.jpg)

You ticket is now submitted, you can enter a reply here when you receive a message from support.

![reply](https://i.imgur.com/LN9H2n4.jpg)

You will find the details of your ticket when you return to the support page.

![ticket status](https://i.imgur.com/70o2V0n.jpg)

To check out the ticket, click on the ticket number.

![checkticket](https://i.imgur.com/AgsD3xe.jpg)
